A note for religious groups

We see a difference between groups whose purpose is to spread or impose religion and groups motivated by their religious belief to work against oppression.

If your group is motivated by religious belief to work against oppression, but doesn't aim to convert people, we would love you to use eConvenor.

Examples of religious groups welcome to use eConvenor include: a feminist Muslim women's group; a Jewish anti-war group; a Buddhist vegetarian food co-op and a Christian group campaigning for refugee rights.
