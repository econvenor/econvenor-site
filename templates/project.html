The eConvenor project

Goal

We provide the eConvenor service to help activist groups around the world be better organised and more effective.

Background

The eConvenor project is a volunteer-run and not-for-profit.

The core of the project are Keira and Sky — two activists from Melbourne, Australia — who were motivated to create eConvenor by decades of watching activist groups struggle to organise themselves and be effective.

The eConvenor service went live in April 2014.
