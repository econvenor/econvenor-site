eConvenor is not your normal web service.

Although other web services will give anyone an account in under a minute, eConvenor works differently.

eConvenor is exclusively for progressive groups, so you must complete an eligibility check and read the terms and conditions before registering an account.

eConvenor is a service for:

  •   grassroots campaigns
  •   non-government organisations (NGOs)
  •   progressive service providers
  •   co-operatives
  •   activist groups and the like.

It takes about 10 minutes to do the eligibility check and read the terms and conditions.

We're excited to offer groups who share our vision of a better world a tool to help them be more effective.
